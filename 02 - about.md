# About Wayaba University of Sri Lanka

Wayaba University of Sri Lanka is committed to excellence in education, research, and community service. Our goal is to create an inclusive learning environment where students and faculty collaborate to solve real-world problems.

## Our Mission
To provide high-quality education and research that empowers students to become skilled, ethical, and engaged citizens.

## Our Values
- Academic excellence
- Integrity and respect
- Innovation and collaboration
- Community engagement

## Contact & Visit
For admissions, programs, or partnership inquiries, please contact the university office or visit the main campus.
